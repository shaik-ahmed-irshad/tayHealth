About Tay Groups

TAY Group was founded in 2002 by its CEO Mrs. Hanen Benabdeladhim and since 20 years, has evolved into a multinational company with operations across Africa, Asia, Europe and the Middle East. Tay Healthcare is a team of healthcare professionals with more than 20 years of experience in the Middle East. We work closely with healthcare facilities, government bodies to deliver high quality services to the healthcare industry. Our motto is to help healthcare facilities and healthcare professionals to identify and capitalize on new and emerging opportunities, provide guidance to ensure successful implementation of changes occurring in the healthcare industry, and work with clients to develop processes that ensure fulfillment of international standards.

We have established a strong reputation for delivering high quality services to healthcare facilities and medical providers in the Middle East. We understand the complexities of the healthcare industry, and we are committed to helping our clients achieve their goals through innovative solutions that are tailored to meet their needs.

We offer a full range of services for healthcare facilities and medical providers, including:

    ~ We offer DHA / MOH / DOH licensing.

    ~ Recruitment ( Nurse , doctor, dental etc. )

    ~ Medical Supplies ( like: Medicine and Equipments )

    ~ Strategic Planning & Consulting

    ~ Operations Management & Administration
